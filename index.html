About Niko & Pipin

Meet Nikola Tessla (named for the energy scientist) & Peregrin Took (namesake of the mischievous hobbit)! They can't wait to show you their best treasure stashes of stolen pens and rubber cat toys!

Why they are finding a new home

One of the wonderful things about ferrets is that new things are fun and exciting! The problem is that when their humans don't have enough time to give them fun new experiences and things to explore, they get bored and unhappy.
