Why are these adorable fuzzies looking for a new home?

One of the wonderful things about ferrets is that new things are fun and exciting! That almost always means they don't get upset or stressed out by change. Unlike many other pets, ferrets think a new place to explore is the best!

The problem is that when their humans don't have enough time to give them fun new experiences and things to explore, they get bored and unhappy.

A little bit about Niko & Pipin's humans; Chuck & Erica

Chuck & Erica work in tech (thus the website) and love bright colors, mischief, adventures, and monkeyshine. But life is always changing and it just isn't possible to have all the fun all the time.

Chuck & Erica have taken these ferrets everywhere - the park, the beach, wine tasting ... and they have ALL had a fantastic time of it. The problem is that it just hasn't been possible in the past year. The fuzzballs are as happy and playful as ever and they love adventure as much as they ever did. So not being able to take them on adventures or play with them as much makes everyone sad.

That's why, even though we love them dearly, we want to them to have a new playground and new friends to play with!

These ferrets come prepared!
Here's a list of their things:

Ferret Cage

  • One large 2-section, 6-level cage with cover and custom ferret litter box
  • Ferret litter mixture
  • Suspended ferret food and water holders

Ferret Play & Travel Gear

  • Ferret play pen with floor and carrying case

  • A rice box full of rice for digging in
  • A crinkle blanket that makes them go a little crazy with happiness

Ferret Food, Treats, & Care Gear

  • Plenty of ferret food and food supplement "fortiflora" for keeping their tummies extra happy
