Why are these adorable fuzzies looking for a new home?

One of the wonderful things about ferrets is that they don't get upset or stressed out by change. Unlike cats, ferrets think a new place to explore is the best! The problem is that when their humans don't have enough time to give them fun new experiences and things to explore, they get bored and unhappy.

Oh yeah, it's that good. See for yourself.
